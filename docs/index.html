About

Hi! I am Leo Wai Yei (or Riley Leo ). I am currently a second year degree student in Asia Pacific University (APU)
studying Computer Science specializing in Data Analytics

I love to learn new things, explore intriguing ideas, and exchange them with like-minded individuals. As a person, I firmly believe that it is never too late to learn, which is why I aspire to be steadfast learn new technologies, revise my way of thinking, and applying them to various situations.

Besides equipping myself with technical skills, I am no stranger to leading, managing, and presenting. Thus, landing myself as the leader and main presenter in most of the projects, assignments, and hackathons.
